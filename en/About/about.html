About Us

Al-Gota Food Company was established in the early nineties of the last century in Eastern Ghouta of Damascus in the Arbin area, starting from the packaging of imported spices and condiments. It expanded by supporting our work in the production of natural dried fruits such as molokhiya and okra... relying on the experience of our ancestors and the advice of medicine. Then we expanded our work by producing... Various jams, cakes, sweets, halva, and tahini. We are distinguished by various types of liquid and solid molasses, dried fruits, and pickles.
The company constantly aims to enhance its exports in local markets, move towards global markets, and introduce its products by participating in international exhibitions related to the company’s products, so that the name of Al-Ghouta Company is associated with quality and excellence, so that we can reach the required level of ambition and exceed the desired desires.
We continue to receive feedback from foreign countries, and this confirms the increasing trend of exporting to these countries. Therefore, we as manufacturers must bear more responsibility in order to maintain the superiority of our local products. We must also maintain our position in the first ranks and work to introduce everything that is New and advanced production lines and expansion of the internal and external marketing staff.
