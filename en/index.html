About Us

Our pride is the experience of our ancestors, we are distinguished by our continuous creativity, our motto is trust and precision, our path to always be better .

Therefore, Al-Ghouta Company was a lush tree. Its first seed was planted in the nineties of the last century, starting from packing local and imported spices and condiments. Those plants grew and expanded by strengthening our work in producing natural dried fruits such as molokhiya, okra, and other natural materials, relying in this on the experience of our ancestors and the advice of medicine. Drying natural materials is the best, healthiest and safest method in the world of nutrition. This company has grown by producing 100% medicinal herbs that are free of chemicals, and various mixtures such as falafel mixture, raw kibbeh mixture, crispy mixture, and various chicken mixtures, in addition to Levantine dishes such as freekeh, kabsa, and kishk. Then we expanded our work by producing various jams, cakes, sweets, halva, and tahini.
